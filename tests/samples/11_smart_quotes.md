# Smart Quotes and Typography Test

This document tests smart quotes and other typographic enhancements.

## Smart Quotes

### Straight to Smart Conversion

"This is a quoted sentence." It should use smart quotes.

'And this is a single-quoted phrase.' It should also be smart.

"Nested 'quotes' should work" correctly.

### Contractions

It's a beautiful day, isn't it? Don't you think so?

We're going to the park. I'll bring the sandwiches.

### Dialogue

"Hello," she said. "How are you?"

"I'm doing well," he replied. "Thank you for asking."

## Dashes and Punctuation

Use an em dash—for emphasis—in your writing.

Use an en dash for ranges like 1–10 or Monday–Friday.

An ellipsis... indicates trailing thought.

## Special Characters

### Currency

The price is $49.99 or €45.00 or £39.99.

### Mathematical

2 × 3 = 6 and 10 ÷ 2 = 5

The temperature is 25°C or 77°F.
